HOW TO PLAY

🎮 BASIC CONTROLS

👆Tap screen / press SPACE — flip gravity between floor and ceiling. Time your flips to dodge obstacles.
✌️Double-tap / hold Power button — trigger Power Blast when meter is full (yellow glow).
⏸Pause button (top right) — pause game and access shop mid-run.

⚡ GRAVITY MECHANIC

⬇️Default: player falls downward toward the floor.
⬆️After tap: player falls upward toward the ceiling.
⏱️There is a short cooldown between flips — shown by the flip bar at bottom.
💡Tip: flip early! Gravity takes time to reverse your momentum.

💎 POWERUPS

💎GEM — +100 score (doubled with combo)
🌀PORTAL — +500 score, warp effect
⭐STAR — 6 seconds invincibility
🛡️SHIELD — extra life (max 5)
⚡BOLT — 3s speed burst + score bonus
🧲MAGNET — auto-collect gems for 5s
💣BOMB — destroys ALL obstacles on screen
🔮CRYSTAL — 3× score multiplier for 7s
❄️FREEZE — slows all enemies for 4s

👾 ENEMIES

🔺SPIKE — static or bobbing triangle on floor/ceiling. Most common.
🟥PILLAR — wall with a gap. Choose floor lane or ceiling lane.
⚡LASER — pulsing beam that blinks on/off. Pass through when dim.
🚁DRONE — flies in a sine wave pattern, fires small shots at you.
🟦TANK — slow heavy enemy that absorbs 3 hits. Drops gems on death.

👹 BOSSES

💀Appear every 3000 points. Bosses track you and fire homing shots. Each zone has a unique boss with different attack patterns.
💥Use Power Blast or Bomb pickups to deal heavy damage.

🔋 POWER METER & HEAT

🔋Power meter fills by 4% per flip. When full — double-tap or press the yellow button to POWER BLAST: nukes obstacles and damages boss.
🌡️Heat meter fills as you get close to obstacles and enemies. Too much heat and you overheat — slowing your flip speed temporarily.

🌊 ZONES

🌆NEON CITY (0–1500) — Classic zone, balanced difficulty.
🌌THE VOID (1500–4000) — Dark zone, enemies faster, more portals.
⛈️STORM (4000–8000) — Lightning hazards, drones appear.
🔥FIRE REALM (8000+) — Maximum chaos: tanks, fire obstacles, boss rush.

🏆 SCORING TIPS

🔗Build COMBO by collecting powerups quickly. Each collect adds 0.5× up to 8× multiplier.
📈Earn XP to level up. Higher level = better score bonuses and shop discounts.
🪙Collect COINS during runs to spend in the shop on permanent upgrades.
🎯Complete ACHIEVEMENTS for massive coin bonuses.
